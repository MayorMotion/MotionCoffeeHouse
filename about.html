About Motion Coffee House

Trusted Partner for Premium Shopping in Yobe State and Beyond

Our Story

Founded in 2025, MotionCoffeeHouse emerged from a simple vision: to bring premium quality products within reach of every household in Damaturu and across Nigeria. What started as a small retail space in Chilla Plaza has grown into Yobe State's most trusted online shopping destination.

We bridge the gap between global quality standards and local accessibility, ensuring that our customers don't have to compromise on excellence regardless of their location.

Our Mission

To revolutionize online shopping of coffee and snacks in Northern Nigeria by providing exceptional quality products, reliable delivery, and outstanding customer service that exceeds expectations.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most customer-centric CoffeeHouse, recognized for trust, quality, and innovation while creating economic opportunities in our community.

Our Core Values

Trust & Reliability

We build lasting relationships through transparency and consistent delivery on our promises.

Quality Excellence

Every product undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets our high standards.

Customer First

Your satisfaction is our priority. We listen, adapt, and go the extra mile for every customer.

Community Impact

We're committed to sustainable growth that benefits our local economy and environment.
